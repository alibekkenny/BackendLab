Project features:

Our web site will be presenting part of the mobile application, where people start working with it. So, it will consist of such parts as:
• Profile: first name, last name, interests and skills, avatar... If not registered, the functionality of the site will not be available until you have an account.
• Monetization part: subscription purchase for extended opportunities, information about its features.
• Installation: page for installation of app and some guide for getting started with it.
